Coffee Orders Data Analysis - Dashboard

Project Overview

The Coffee Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of coffee sales across different countries, customer segments, and time periods. This report is designed to help business stakeholders identify key trends, optimize sales strategies, and make data-driven decisions.

Business Problem Addressed

The report aims to solve the following business challenges:

  • Understanding sales trends over time to identify peak seasons.
  • Analyzing sales performance by country to allocate marketing and supply chain resources effectively.
  • Identifying top customers to improve retention strategies.
  • Evaluating the impact of roast type, loyalty cards, and product size on overall sales.

Data Sources

The dashboard uses sales transaction data extracted from coffeeOrdersData.xlsx. The dataset includes:

  • Order date
  • Roast type (Dark, Light, Medium)
  • Loyalty card usage
  • Product size (0.2 Kg, 0.5 Kg, 1.0 Kg, 2.5 Kg)
  • Country of purchase
  • Customer details
  • Sales amounts

Steps Taken

1. Data Preparation

  • Imported the dataset into Power BI/Excel.
  • Checked for missing values and inconsistencies.
  • Formatted columns correctly (e.g., Date fields, Sales as currency).
  • Standardized country names to avoid duplicates.

2. Data Transformation

  • Created a date table for time-based analysis.
  • Extracted year, month, and quarter for trend analysis.
  • Cleaned and normalized customer names to avoid duplicates.
  • Applied filters for product size, loyalty card usage, and roast type.

3. Data Modeling

  • Established relationships between Sales Data, Date Table, and Customer Table.
  • Optimized the model to ensure efficient calculations.

Key DAX Formulas

1. Total Sales Calculation

        Total Sales = SUM(Sales[Amount])

Adds up all sales transactions.

2. Sales by Country

        Sales by Country = CALCULATE(SUM(Sales[Amount]), ALLEXCEPT(Sales, Sales[Country]))

Computes sales figures for each country.

3. Monthly Sales Growth

        Sales Growth % = 
        VAR PrevMonthSales = CALCULATE(SUM(Sales[Amount]), PREVIOUSMONTH(Date[Date]))
        RETURN 
        IF(PrevMonthSales = 0, BLANK(), (SUM(Sales[Amount]) - PrevMonthSales) / PrevMonthSales)

Calculates percentage growth in sales compared to the previous month.

Business Problems Solved by the Coffee Sales Dashboard

The Coffee Sales Dashboard was designed to address key business challenges faced by the company in managing and analyzing sales data. Below are the primary business problems it solves:

1. Identifying Sales Trends Over Time

Problem: The business lacked clear insights into how coffee sales fluctuated over time, making it difficult to anticipate peak seasons and adjust marketing efforts accordingly.

Solution: The "Total Sales Over Time" visualization helps identify:

  • Seasonal sales patterns.
  • Growth trends and declines.
  • Peak months for coffee sales, enabling better inventory planning.

2. Understanding Regional Sales Performance

Problem: The company did not have a clear view of which regions were driving the most revenue, leading to ineffective resource allocation in marketing and supply chain.

Solution: The "Sales by Country" dashboard shows:

  • Which countries contribute the most revenue.
  • The United States as the dominant market, followed by Ireland and the UK.
  • Opportunities for expansion or increased marketing efforts in underperforming regions.

3. Customer Loyalty & High-Value Customers

Problem: The business needed a way to identify its most valuable customers to improve retention strategies.

Solution: The "Top 5 Customers" dashboard:

  • Highlights high-value customers based on purchase history.
  • Helps the business implement loyalty programs and personalized marketing.
  • Encourages targeted promotions for repeat customers.

4. Evaluating Product Preferences (Roast Type, Size, Loyalty Card)

Problem: The company lacked insights into which product variations (roast type, size) were most preferred by customers, making demand forecasting difficult.

Solution:

  • Roast Type Analysis: Understand if customers prefer Dark, Medium, or Light roasts.
  • Size Selection Analysis: Provides insights into which product sizes (0.2 Kg, 0.5 Kg, 1.0 Kg, 2.5 Kg) sell the most.
  • Loyalty Card Usage: Reveals if customers with a loyalty card make higher purchases.

5. Improving Business Decision-Making

Problem: Decision-makers previously relied on intuition rather than data-driven insights.

Solution: With interactive filters and real-time data visualizations, business leaders can now:

  • Make informed decisions based on historical sales data.
  • Optimize marketing and promotions for specific customer segments.
  • Improve supply chain management based on demand trends.
