British Airways Review Dashboard

Project Overview

The British Airways Review Dashboard is an interactive analytics tool designed to analyze customer reviews and satisfaction metrics for British Airways. It provides valuable insights into customer experience across multiple categories, including:

  • Seat Comfort
  • Cabin Staff Service
  • Entertainment
  • Food Quality
  • Overall Rating

The goal is to identify trends, pinpoint areas for improvement, and enhance service quality based on passenger feedback.

Business Problems Addressed

  • Understanding Customer Satisfaction Trends - Track sentiment trends across different service categories.
  • Assessing Aircraft Comfort Levels - Compare comfort scores across different aircraft models.
  • Geographic Trends in Reviews - Analyze seat comfort ratings by country.
  • Tracking Review Volume - Identify which aircraft receive the most/least reviews.
  • Detecting Areas for Improvement - Highlight underperforming service areas.

Steps Taken in the Analysis

1. Data Preparation

  • Collected and cleaned customer review data.
  • Standardized date formats and numerical ratings.
  • Created mapping for geographic review trends.

2. Data Transformation

Built a calculated field to allow dynamic metric selection based on user input:

CASE [Pick a metric]
WHEN 'Overall Rating' THEN [Rating]
WHEN 'Cabin Staff Services' THEN [Cabin Staff Service]
WHEN 'Entertainment' THEN [Entertainment]
WHEN 'Food' THEN [Food Beverages]
WHEN 'Ground Services' THEN [Ground Service]
WHEN 'Seat Comfort' THEN [Seat Comfort]
WHEN 'Value' THEN [Value For Money]
END

3. Data Modeling

  • Established relationships between aircraft types, reviews, and customer satisfaction.
  • Optimized Tableau calculations for better performance.

Dashboard Components

Key Metrics (KPIs)

  • Average Overall Rating
  • Cabin Staff Service Score
  • Value for Money
  • Food & Beverage Score
  • Entertainment Score
  • Seat Comfort Score
  • Ground Service Rating

Data Visualizations

  • Average Seat Comfort by Month - Identifies trends over time.
  • Average Seat Comfort by Country - Map visualization of customer satisfaction.
  • Average Seat Comfort by Aircraft - Comparison of aircraft models based on comfort ratings.

Key Findings & Insights

1. Seat Comfort Varies by Aircraft

A380 has the highest seat comfort rating (3.3), while A319 and A320 have lower scores.

Recommendation: Optimize seating configurations on lower-rated aircraft.

2. Service Areas Need Improvement

Food & Beverages (2.4) and Entertainment (1.4) received the lowest ratings.

Recommendation: Enhance in-flight meals and upgrade entertainment options.

3. Geographic Trends in Customer Satisfaction

Some countries report lower satisfaction scores, indicating service disparities.

Recommendation: Target low-rated regions for service enhancements.

4. Review Volume Insights

Certain aircraft, like the Boeing 747 and A380, receive significantly more reviews.

Recommendation: Encourage more feedback on underrepresented aircraft types.

Actionable Recommendations

  • Enhance In-Flight Services - Improve food quality and expand menu choices.
  • Upgrade Entertainment - Offer more diverse content.
  • Optimize Seating Experience - Reconfigure seating for better comfort.
  • Target Regional Improvements - Improve service in low-rated regions.
  • Encourage More Customer Feedback - Gather more insights from passengers.

Conclusion

The British Airways Review Dashboard provides valuable insights into passenger experiences by analyzing customer reviews. This tool helps:

  • Track service quality trends
  • Identify weak areas
  • Optimize airline operations to enhance passenger satisfaction.
