Key DAX Formulas & Measures

Essential calculations for dashboard insights.

1. Total Sales

    Total_Sales = SUM(Sales[Total Amount])

Purpose: Computes total revenue from all transactions.

2. Total Quantity Sold

    Total_Quantity = SUM(Sales[Quantity])

Purpose: Measures the total number of mobile units sold.

3. Average Selling Price

    Avg_Price = DIVIDE([Total_Sales], [Total_Quantity])

Purpose: Calculates the average price per unit.

4. Total Sales (Same Period Last Year - SPLY)

    Total_SPLY = CALCULATE([Total_Sales], SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR(Date[Date]))

Purpose: Compares the current year‚Äôs sales with the same period last year.

5. Running Total Sales (MTD)

    MTD_Sales = CALCULATE([Total_Sales], DATESYTD(Date[Date]))

Purpose: Shows cumulative sales within a month.
