Car Sales Analysis Dashboard


Overview

This project is a non-traditional interactive car sales dashboard built in Power BI. It enables users to analyze car sales performance through an engaging, modern visual approach rather than conventional reports. The dashboard incorporates high-quality car images, interactive filters, and dynamic visual storytelling.

Dashboard Features

  • Track total sales, growth rate, and pre-booking statistics.
  • Dynamic car images allow users to interact with different models.
  • Toggle between 2019-2022 to analyze yearly trends.
  • Filter sales by Direct Sale, Third Party, and Pre-Book.
  • Analyze customer segments: Replacement, Servicing, and Other categories.
  • Custom DAX formulas power real-time calculations and insights.

Car Sales Analysis Dashboard

Car Sales Analysis Dashboard

This project offers a detailed analysis of car sales trends, emphasizing vehicle availability based on year and fuel type. By examining historical data, we can identify patterns in consumer demand, shifts in fuel preferences, and market fluctuations. These insights enable car dealerships and manufacturers to optimize inventory management, reduce overstock or shortages, and align their offerings with emerging trends. With a data-driven approach, businesses can make strategic decisions that enhance sales performance, improve supply chain efficiency, and stay competitive in an ever-evolving automotive industry.

Why We Are Doing This

The automotive industry is highly competitive and constantly evolving, driven by shifting consumer preferences, technological advancements, and economic factors. With the growing demand for electric vehicles, changing fuel prices, and seasonal sales trends, manufacturers and dealerships must adapt quickly to stay ahead. By analyzing historical car availability data, this project helps stakeholders make informed decisions by identifying market trends, optimizing inventory, and predicting consumer demand. Understanding how availability varies by year and fuel type allows businesses to align their strategies with industry shifts, ensuring efficiency and profitability in an ever-changing market.

Dashboard Sections Explained

Car Selection Panel

  • Luxury SUV: High-end SUV sales analysis.
  • Sports Hybrid: Performance-based hybrid sales.
  • Classic Vintage: Tracks vintage car sales.

Sales Performance Section

This section provides a comprehensive overview of key sales KPIs, including total revenue, pre-sales performance, and budget tracking Easily monitor how sales are progressing, compare actual performance against targets, and gain insights to drive better business decisions.

Yearly Sales Toggle

Users can seamlessly switch between 2019, 2020, 2021, and 2022 to compare yearly sales trends. This feature provides a clear year-over-year analysis helping to identify growth patterns, seasonal trends, and overall business performance at a glance.

Sales Type Breakdown

This section provides a clear visualization of Direct Sales, Third-Party Sales, and Pre-Book Sales, helping you understand the contribution of each sales type. Easily compare their performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions to optimize your sales strategy.

Customer Segmentation

Displays sales performance across Replacement, Servicing, and Other categories.

How to Use

  • Filter by sales type or customer segment for deeper insights.
