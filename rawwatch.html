DAX Formulas Explanations

Sales Calculations

Total Sales

Total_Sales = SUM(Orders[Sales])

Explanation: Adds up all the sales values from the Orders table. Used to calculate overall revenue.

Current Year Sales

Current_Year_Sales = CALCULATE(SUM(Orders[Sales]), DATESYTD(Orders[Order Date]))

Explanation: Calculates total sales for the current year using DATESYTD for the year-to-date period.

Previous Year Sales

Previous_Year_Sales = CALCULATE(SUM(Orders[Sales]), YEAR(Orders[Order Date]) = 2021)

Explanation: Fetches total sales for the previous year. Helps in year-over-year growth calculations.

Sales Growth (%)

Sales_Growth = DIVIDE(([Current_Year_Sales] - [Previous_Year_Sales]), [Previous_Year_Sales])

Explanation: Measures the percentage increase or decrease in sales. Formula: (Current Sales - Previous Sales) / Previous Sales.

Category & Segment Assignments

Category Switch Formula

Category_Switch = 
VAR cate = SELECTEDVALUE(Orders[Category])
VAR _type = SWITCH(TRUE(), 
    cate = "Office Supplies", "Digital Watch", 
    cate = "Furniture", "Analog Watch", 
    cate = "Technology", "Other")
RETURN _type

Explanation: Assigns custom product types based on their category. Ensures consistency in reporting.

Segment Switch Formula

New_Categ = 
VAR cate = New_Segment[Segment]
VAR _type = SWITCH(cate, 
    "Consumer", "Digital Watch", 
    "Home Office", "Analog Watch", 
    "Corporate", "Mechanical Watch")
RETURN _type

Explanation: Maps different business segments to specific watch types.
