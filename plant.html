Plant Co. Gross Profit Performance Dashboard

Overview

This Power BI report provides insights into Plant Co.'s Gross Profit Performance for the year 2023, helping stakeholders understand profitability trends across different months, countries, and product categories.

Purpose of the Project

The primary purpose of this project is to analyze year-to-date (YTD) gross profit, compare it to the prior year-to-date (PYTD), and segment profitability by country, product type, and other dimensions.

Business Problems Solved

  • Identifies Profit Performance by Country: Highlights countries with the highest and lowest profitability.
  • Year-to-Date (YTD) vs Prior Year-to-Date (PYTD): Compares current year performance with the previous year.
  • Monthly Gross Profit Trends: Tracks gross profit fluctuations over time.
  • Profitability Segmentation: Analyzes Gross Profit Percentage (GP%) and total gross profit to highlight key insights.

Key Features

  • Dynamic titles and visuals reflecting selected filters.
  • Comparison of YTD vs. PYTD for Gross Profit, Sales, and Quantity.
  • Profitability segmentation by GP%.

Key Performance Metrics

üîπ Gross Profit Percentage (GP%)

        GP% = DIVIDE([Gross Profit], [Sales])

‚úî Calculates the profitability ratio.

üîπ PYTD Gross Profit

        PYTD_GrossProfit = CALCULATE([Gross Profit], SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR(Dim_Date[Date]), Dim_Date[InPast] = TRUE)

‚úî Retrieves Gross Profit from the corresponding period last year.

üîπ PYTD Quantity and Sales

        PYTD_Quantity = CALCULATE([Quantity], SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR(Dim_Date[Date]), Dim_Date[InPast] = TRUE)
        PYTD_Sales = CALCULATE([Sales], SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR(Dim_Date[Date]), Dim_Date[InPast] = TRUE)

‚úî Ensures accurate year-over-year comparisons.

üîπ Switching Contexts for YTD and PYTD

        S_YTD = SWITCH(Selected_Values, 
            "Sales", [YTD_Sales], 
            "Quantity", [YTD_Quantity], 
            "Gross Profit", [YTD_GrossProfit], 
            BLANK()
        )

‚úî Enables dynamic selection of metrics for better interactivity.

üîπ YTD vs PYTD Comparison

        YTD vs PYTD = [S_YTD] - [S_PYTD]

‚úî Measures variance to track growth or decline over time.
